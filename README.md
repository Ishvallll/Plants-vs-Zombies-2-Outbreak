# Plants-vs-Zombies-2-Outbreak
**Plants vs Zombies 2: Outbreak**

# WARNING! this fan-game still under development, so please, wait till its done.

Plants vs Zombies 2: Outbreak is a fan-game inspired by *Plants vs Zombies 2*, This game does not replace the original title — it expands its universe, experiments with new ideas, and pushes the classic PvZ formula into unexplored territory.

The core philosophy of Outbreak is simple: **more of everything**.

The game introduces **new plants** with original mechanics, unusual interactions, and strategies that go beyond the traditional PvZ gameplay loop. Alongside them come **new zombies**, including exclusive variants with unique behaviors that force players to adapt and rethink every defense.

Outbreak features **multiple minigames**, ranging from fast-paced challenges to experimental modes that focus on reflexes, logic, and pure survival. To amplify the chaos, the game adds **new power-ups**, opening the door to unpredictable and highly dynamic matches.

Exploration is expanded through **new maps**, each with distinct themes, mechanics, and rules, ensuring that no match feels repetitive. For players who want complete freedom, the game includes a **Sandbox mode**, allowing experimentation and creative setups far beyond standard PvZ limits.

Community is a central pillar of Outbreak. The project supports **mods**, encouraging players to create and share custom content. A dedicated **Discord server** serves as a hub for updates, ideas, and community interaction. The game is also directly connected to **Fire Studio**, extending the project beyond a single game and into a broader creative ecosystem.

Plants vs Zombies 2: Outbreak is not just a fan-game.
It is a creative playground, a controlled outbreak of ideas, and a tribute to the PvZ universe — where plants, zombies, and experimentation are allowed to go too far.
